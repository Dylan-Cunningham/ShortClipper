ShortClipper

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 26, 2026

Overview

ShortClipper is a local desktop video clipping tool. It is designed to process user-provided video files, transcripts, clip plans, and generated clips on the user's own computer.

Information Stored Locally

ShortClipper may store the following information on the user's computer:

  • Local video file paths selected by the user.
  • Generated transcripts and transcript timing files.
  • Reviewed clip candidates, edited words, and clip metadata.
  • Rendered video clips and saved clip library files.
  • Local publishing queue details such as captions and hashtags.

Third-Party Uploads

ShortClipper does not upload video clips to third-party services unless the user explicitly connects an account and chooses to upload selected clips. When a user chooses to upload to TikTok or another service, that service may process the uploaded video and related caption data according to its own privacy policy.

Credentials

If TikTok upload support is enabled, access tokens and related credentials should be stored locally on the user's computer and used only to perform actions requested by the user.

Contact

For questions about ShortClipper, contact the app owner through the associated GitHub repository.
